# S1 D1 T0 - Spiral + DEP, Not Dead/Live

Status: populated.

These papers use spiral geometry or spiral DEP concepts and DEP, but they do not directly target live/dead or viable/nonviable sorting.

Included examples:

- Betyar and Ramiar 2026: OpenFOAM spiral DEP separation of RBC and MDA-MB-231 cells.
- Patel and Xuan 2011: curvature-induced DEP in spiral microchannels.
- Yilmaz 2010 thesis: spiral channel DEP separator.

Use in project: geometry and electrode precedent.
